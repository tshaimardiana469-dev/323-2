About Weilian Intelligent Finance

Company Background

Weilian Intelligent Financial Information Consulting Co., Ltd. is located in Pool Valley, Brighton & Hove, UK. Founded by a team of senior economists, data analysts and international financial consultants, we focus on breaking information barriers in the global financial industry.

We strictly comply with UK financial consulting industry regulatory standards, provide transparent and objective data research services, and refuse biased market guidance to protect every client’s investment interests.

Our Core Values

1. Accuracy: All market data and analysis reports are double verified by professional analysts.

2. Customization: One-on-one consulting solutions tailored to client asset scale and risk preference.

3. Integrity: Independent third-party consulting without cooperation bias with any financial institutions.

4. Global Vision: Cover European, American, Asian and emerging market financial information resources.
